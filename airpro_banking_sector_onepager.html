Why Banking Sector Needs This
Vault & record rooms require precise temperature control — data loss and document damage are costly
Server/data center cooling failure leads to downtime and regulatory risk
AC running in empty branches (off-hours) wastes millions in electricity annually across Lahore
Fire & smoke risks in server rooms and cash storage areas require instant detection

Lahore summer peak (May–Sep): Outdoor temperatures reach 42–48°C. Banks spend heavily on cooling. AIRPro adjusts AC based on outdoor temp + indoor occupancy — cutting waste automatically.

Our 3-Module Solution

AIRPro — Smart AC Management

Remote monitoring & automated control of all AC units. Occupancy sensors turn AC ON when staff/customers arrive, OFF when premises are empty. Outdoor temp feed adjusts setpoints automatically. Non-invasive — no AC modification needed.

IRIS Ultra — Temperature & Humidity

Precision sensors for vaults, server rooms, record rooms & ATM cabins. Alerts triggered instantly if conditions breach safe thresholds. Protects cash, documents, and IT equipment from humidity damage.

FireSense — Fire, Smoke & CO₂ Detection

Real-time fire, smoke and CO₂ detection. Instant SMS/email/mobile alerts to branch managers and security. Critical for server rooms, generator rooms, and currency vaults.

How AC Automation Works for Banks
Occupancy-Based
Motion/PIR sensor detects presence → AC turns ON automatically → Branch empty → AC turns OFF
Weather-Based
Outdoor temp feed (Lahore) → AI adjusts AC setpoint → Prevents over-cooling on mild days
Schedule-Based
Bank hours 9AM–5PM → Auto on/off schedules → No AC running after closing time
Central Dashboard
All Lahore branches visible on one screen → Branch-wise energy reports → Fault alerts
